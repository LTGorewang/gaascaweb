Ways in which corporations can support (Sponsor)

  1. A bigger and safer bus which is wheelchair friendly as our present bus has became a liability and a mechanical risk as we are not able to transport our meals-on-wheels beneficiaries to and from the centre for the daily programmes.   3. Renovation of old dilapidated rooms in order to address the backlog of applicants (waiting list) for accommodation.
  4. Our change rooms/toilets need to be retiled
  5. Part paving in front of the living units in order to reduce dust and slippery conditions in windy and rainy days
